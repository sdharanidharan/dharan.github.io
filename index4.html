About Paris

Eiffel Tower: The symbol of Paris and a must-visit landmark, offering breathtaking views of the city from its observation decks.

Louvre Museum: One of the world's largest and most famous art museums, housing a vast collection, including the iconic Mona Lisa.

Notre-Dame Cathedral: A masterpiece of Gothic architecture, known for its stunning facade and intricate stained glass windows.
